Tabme — FAQ

Chrome extension for organizing bookmarks and tabs, done right.

Q: What is Tabme, and how does it work?

A: Tabme is a Chrome extension designed to make managing your tabs and bookmarks effortless. You can group tabs into folders, use drag-and-drop functionality to arrange them, and search across opened, saved, archived, and recently closed tabs. Tabme also supports color-coding, archiving, dark mode, and keyboard accessibility to enhance your browsing experience.

Q: How syncing saved bookmarks works?

A: Syncing saved bookmarks with chrome bookmarks and across devices is in progress. This is top-requested feature, that will be delivered soon. Stay tuned :)

Q. How do I save and organize my tabs?

A: To save your tabs, simply drag and drop them from left sidebar into a any create folder. if you drag-and-and tab into "New Folder" then new Folder will be created automatically. You can create multiple folders, assign colors to them for easy identification, and arrange your tabs in the way that best suits your workflow. You can also use the "Shelve" feature to save all open tabs into a single folder.

Q. Can I restore all tabs from a folder at once?

A: Yes! With the "Open All Tabs" feature, you can instantly restore all the tabs saved in a specific folder. This is particularly useful when you want to reopen a group of shelved tabs.

Q. How search functionality works?

A: The search to quickly find any tab—whether it's currently open, saved, archived, or recently closed. It's acceable from keyboard. 1) You can activate search input by TAB key. 1) You can start typing immediatly if the page already in focused.

Q: What happens to my tabs when I archive them?

A: Archiving tabs in Tabme is a way to clean up your working space tabs while keeping them accessible for future use. Archived tabs are available during search and when you click "show archived" button.

Q: Does Tabme support dark mode?

A: Absolutely! You can switch between light and dark modes by clicking "settings" button.

Q: Is there keyboard accessibility support?

A: Yes, Tabme is designed with keyboard accessibility in mind. You can navigate your tabs only using keyboard. Open newtab in browser; press TAB key to focus page; and use arrow keys to navigate all bookmarks.

Q: Is Tabme really free to use?

A: Yes, Tabme is completely free to use. Enjoy it without any hidden fees or subscriptions! If you like the extension I would welcome a positive review on the ChromeStore.
