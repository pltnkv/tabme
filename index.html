Tabme Chrome Extension

Organizing bookmarks and browser tabs

Tabme is a tab and bookmark manager that helps you organize your projects.
Boost your productivity — turn chaotic browsing into a structured experience.
Keep all your documents one click away on one screen.

Tabme is entirely free to use.


Key features:

👆 Simple Interface With Intuitive Drag-and-Drop

Save your tabs effortlessly by dragging and dropping them into a Folder.
Сolor coding and arranging in space helps find saved tabs instantly.

📦 Stash Open Tabs

Save all your open tabs into a folder by clicking "Stash all Tabs".
Use "Open All" in the Folder menu to restore all saved Tabs.

🧹 Close Duplicated tabs

Close the duplicate tabs with a single click.

🚀 Performance-Driven

Works fast even with hundreds of saved tabs.

🔎 Smart Search Functionality

Seamlessly search across open, saved, archived, and recently closed tabs.

🌙 Dark Mode Support

Switch to dark mode for comfortable viewing at night or in low-light environments.

⌨️ Keyboard Accessibility Support

Manage bookmarks and tabs using keyboard shortcuts for faster navigation.

⭐️ Instant Tab Access

Replace your traditional new tab page with Tabme for instant access to all your saved tabs.
There is an alternative "Tabme — version without newtab" app. It has all the same features but without taking over "Chrome newtab".
