Frequently asked questions

Tabme is a Chrome extension designed to make managing your tabs and bookmarks effortless.
You can save tabs into folders and spaces, use drag-and-drop functionality to arrange them.
Enjoy a clean, easy-to-navigate design that keeps your focus on what matters.

Tabme overrides the Chrome new tab page to provide quick access to your saved tabs and folders. This gives you immediate access to your organized tabs whenever you open a new tab.

If you prefer to keep your default new tab page, opt for the "Tabme Mini" extension available on the Chrome Web Store. It includes all the same features but doesn’t open on every new tab.

Your data remains on your device — nothing is stored in the cloud.
All usage analytics are completely anonymous.
We never sell or share your data with anyone.
