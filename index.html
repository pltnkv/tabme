Tabme Chrome Extension

Organizing bookmarks and tabs, done right.

Install Tabme In Chrome Store


The Tabme was built with a frictionless user experience in mind.
Effortlessly save browser tabs and navigate across them.
Use colors and arranging in space to find your bookmarks instantly.

Tabme is entirely free to use.


Key Features:

⚡️ One-Click Access
Quickly open any saved tab with a single click.
Experience the most efficient UX for managing tabs and bookmarks.

📦 Shelve Open Tabs
Save all your open tabs into a folder by clicking "Shelve all Tabs"
and restore them later by clicking "Open All Tabs".

👆 Intuitive Drag-and-Drop
Save your tabs effortlessly. Simply drag and drop, and organize them the way you need.

🚀 Performance-Driven
Enjoy lightning-fast performance, even when managing hundreds of tabs simultaneously.

🔎 Smart Search Functionality
Seamlessly search across open, saved, archived, and recently closed tabs.

💡 Minimalistic Interface
Work distraction-free with a clean and clear interface designed for productivity.

🌈 Color-Coded Efficiency
Use color-coded tabs and folders to categorize and locate your bookmarks faster.

📦 Archive Instead of Delete
Archive tabs to keep your workspace tidy while retaining access to important bookmarks.

🌙 Dark Mode Support
Switch to dark mode for comfortable viewing at night or in low-light environments.

⌨️ Keyboard Accessibility Support
Manage bookmarks and tabs using keyboard shortcuts for faster navigation.

⭐️ Instant Tab Access
Replace your traditional new tab page with Tabme for instant access to all your saved tabs.
