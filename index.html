Tabme Chrome Extension

Organizing bookmarks and tabs, done right.

Install Tabme In Chrome Store


The Tabme was built with a frictionless user experience in mind.
Effortlessly save tabs and navigate across them.
Use colors and arranging in space to find your bookmarks instantly.

Tabme is entirely free to use.


Key Features:

⚡️ One-Click Access: Open any saved Tab with a single click. The most frictionless UX of Tabs & Bookmarks management.

📦 Shelve open Tabs: Save opened Tabs into a Folder and Restore them by clicking "Open All Tabs".

👆 Intuitive Drag-and-Drop: Save your tabs effortlessly. Simply drag and drop, and organize them the way you need.

🚀 Performance-Driven: Enjoy lightning-fast responsiveness, even when managing hundreds of tabs.

🔎 Smart Search Functionality: Seamlessly search across opened, saved, archived, and recently closed tabs.

💡 Minimalistic Interface: Experience an interface that prioritizes clarity. No distractions, just quick access to your tabs.

🌈 Color-Coded Efficiency: Use colors to your advantage. Speed up your search by visually categorizing and identifying tabs faster.

📦 Archive Instead of Delete: Keep your saved tabs up-to-date by archiving rather than deleting them. Easily find archived tabs via search whenever you need them.

🌙 Supports Dark Mode theme: Have comfortable viewing at night.

⌨️ Keyboard accessibility support.: Manage Bookmarks without mouse.

⭐️ Instant Tab Access: Tabme replaces traditional "new tab", granting you instant access to all your saved tabs upon launch. This is an optional feature. You can always change “newtab” in Chrome settings: "Settings" → "On start-up" → "Open the New Tab page".
