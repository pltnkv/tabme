Tabme vs. Toby:
Why Switch to Tabme?

1) Tabme is 100% Free

All of Tabme’s features are free, while Toby’s premium options can limit your experience.

2) Clean and Simple UX

With Tabme’s uncluttered design, finding bookmarks is easier and faster.
Toby’s UI can feel heavy and distracting.

3) Focused on Content, Not UI

Tabme maximizes visible content with less scrolling, so you get find bookmarks without extra clicks.

4) Blazing Fast Performance

Even with hundreds of bookmarks, Tabme remains fast and responsive,
unlike Toby, which can become laggy very quickly.

5) Constant Updates

Tabme is actively developed and regularly adds new features to meet users' needs.

6) Easy Bookmark Import

Switching is easy!

  1. Export bookmarks from Toby as a JSON file: Account -> Export -> JSON.
  2. In Tabme, go to Settings -> Import from Toby JSON to load your bookmarks.
